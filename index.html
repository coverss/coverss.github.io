For more than 40 years, The Original Pita Stop restaurant in its Southside location has been known for our Mediterranean and American Cuisine. Only the finest meats and freshest vegetables, which come fresh from local farmer's markets, find their way into our kitchen. From our signature Shish Kabobs served with hefty portions of Lamb, Steak, or Shrimp, to our fresh daily Seafood specials, you'll find the most delectable ingredients for your palate.
